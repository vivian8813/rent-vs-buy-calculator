Rent vs buy in Australia: what usually drives the result

In Australia, the rent vs buy decision is often dominated by mortgage interest rates, stamp duty and closing costs, and local property taxes. Your expected time in the home is usually the single biggest factor‚Äîshort stays often make renting cheaper, while longer stays can favor buying if equity and appreciation outweigh ownership costs.

How to use this Australia rent vs buy calculator

  1. Start with your expected stay. Try 3, 5, 7, and 10 years to see how break-even changes.
  2. Set a realistic mortgage rate. Even small changes can shift the outcome.
  3. Include stamp duty. Use "Buying closing costs" to approximate provincial stamp duty.
  4. Use a conservative investment return. This represents opportunity cost of cash.

Common Canadian assumptions to sanity-check

  • Property tax varies by municipality and can materially change costs.
  • Maintenance scales with home price‚Äîolder homes often cost more to maintain.
  • Condo fees can be substantial in condos and some strata properties.
  • Land transfer tax varies by province and can significantly affect upfront costs.
